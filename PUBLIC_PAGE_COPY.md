# TradeMirrorOS Public Copy

## Product One-Liner

TradeMirrorOS is a local-first operating layer that turns trading experience into durable memory.

## Short Showcase Version

TradeMirrorOS is built for traders who do not want their best lessons to disappear into short chat context.

It preserves plans, executions, reviews, mistakes, recovered edges, and behavior patterns as structured memory that can be searched, revisited, and reused over time.

## Why It Resonates

- it treats memory as infrastructure, not an afterthought
- it helps a trader see repeated mistakes and valid paths more clearly
- it is local-first by default and keeps publishing under explicit control
- it is already live on ClawHub for public discovery

## Brand Framing

- `Trade`: grounded in real trading behavior
- `Mirror`: reflects your decisions, biases, mistakes, and valid paths back to you
- `OS`: acts as a long-horizon operating layer, not a one-off helper tool

## What It Is Not

- not a signal-selling room
- not an auto-trading bot
- not a copy-trading product
- not a generic note archive
